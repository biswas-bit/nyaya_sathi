Frequently Asked Questions

What is digital nyaya sathi?

Digital Nyaya Sathi is a platform that provides legal advice through a chatbot, speeds up FIR filing, allows anonymous crime reporting, and offers support for tracking and managing legal cases..

What happens when I report anonymously?

If you report anonymously on Digital Nyaya Sathi, your identity is not shared with anyone, including authorities or the public. The system forwards your report without attaching your name or contact, ensuring your privacy and safety while still helping take action..

How do you help for easy FIR?

Digital Nyaya Sathi helps with easy FIR filing by guiding users step-by-step to fill out required details and auto-generating a draft FIR. It also provides instructions on where and how to submit it, saving time and reducing confusion.

How to connect with Lawyers?

To connect with lawyers on Digital Nyaya Sathi, users select their legal issue, and the app shows a list of verified lawyers based on expertise and location. They can then view profiles and contact the lawyer directly through call, message, or email..

Is my Personal Information Safe?

Yes, your personal information is safe on Digital Nyaya Sathi. The platform uses data encryption and shares your details only with your consent, ensuring full privacy and security.
